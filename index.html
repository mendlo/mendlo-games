About the game

Miners, Moos & Miracles was built from the ground up as an incremental idle game, wrapped in a nostalgic CRT-styled shell. Manage a small homestead (gold, barley, milk, eggs, and more), then hop between a handful of themed mini-games tied together by the same world.

  • ☠Dark Mine idle tower defense
  • 🍴Cooking whip up dishes for the homestead
  • ⚽Cow Ball the barnyard's favorite sport
  • 🐔Chicken Coop keep the flock happy
  • 👕Wardrobe customize your character
