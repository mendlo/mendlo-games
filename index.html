About the game

Miners, Moos & Miracles is built from the ground up as an incremental idle game, wrapped in a nostalgic retro-styled shell. Manage a small homestead (gold, barley, milk, eggs, and more), then hop between a handful of themed mini-games tied together by the same world.

  • 🌾Farm & Farmhouse work the fields, then head inside
  • ☠Dark Mine idle tower defense
  • ⚽Cow Ball the barnyard's favorite sport
  • 🐔Chicken Coop catch eggs before they hit the ground
  • 🐟Fish Tank feed a growing school of fish
  • 🍴Cooking whip up dishes for the homestead
  • 👕Wardrobe customize your character
  • ⚔Hero Fighter battle demons for shards
